Regular Potato

Description

This is a regular potato. Nothing irregular about it

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • I can't believe it is not butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Wash potato
  2. Poke 12 holes in potato
  3. Cook potato for 1 potato time in microwave
  4. Cut potato in middle with knife and ensure potato is sufficiently cooked
  5. Add butter
  6. Add salt
  7. Add pepper
  8. Cut potato out of skin
  9. Eat potato
